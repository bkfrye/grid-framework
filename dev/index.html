At C2, test prep is different.

Most other standardized test preparation programs are one-size-fits-all. They have large class sizes, and are often scheduled too close to students’ test dates for them to see significant improvement. But C2 creates customized academic plans in order to meet each student’s needs, and conducts our tutoring in a small-group setting that runs as long as you need it to.


C2 test preparation:

Strengthens core reading, writing and math skills
Teaches foundational, lifelong learning strategies
Helps students balance test prep and school work
Guides students through their college admissions


Our method is simple. An initial full-length diagnostic test determines your strong suits and your weaknesses, and then we craft a study plan to fit. Students learn tips and tricks to master individual tests from the SATs to the APs, but they also learn skills that will serve them across their whole academic career.
